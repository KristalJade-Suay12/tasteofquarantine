Tempura Sardine

Ingredients:
3 cans Sardines
1 pc eggs
3/4 cup Water
2 tbsp Soy Sauce
1/2 cup Flour
Cooking oil
Procedure:
1. Drain the oil from sardines.
2. Then break an egg into a bowl and whip it until well mixed.
At that point add 3/4 cup of water and 2 tablespoons of soy sauce to the egg and mix everything together.
As you continue this mixing, blend in (a little at a time) 1/2 cup of whole wheat flour to form a smooth batter.
3. Now dredge (very carefully, so they don't fall apart) your fish in some additional flour (so, later on, the batter will stick to them).
4. Then put 1/2 cup of oil into the frying pan, and heat all the shortening to a moderate temperature.
5. When the oil is hot, dip the sardines in the batter and fry them until they're brown (about 3 minutes per side).
If you wish, you can spoon extra batter over each fish before you turn it
6. Remove the fried sardines from the skillet, drain and serve.
