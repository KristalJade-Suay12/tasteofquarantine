Tuna Nuggets

Ingredients:
1 can tuna
Bread Crumbs or 2 packs of crackers
1 pc Onion
Salt and Ground Black Pepper to taste
Procedure:
1. If you’ll be using crackers, crush the crackers while it is still inside the pack using a rolling pin any hard object.
2. In a large bowl, add the bread crumbs or crackers, tuna flakes, chopped onions, and all-purpose flour. Mix them well.
3. Add salt and pepper to the mixture and mix until all the ingredients are well-incorporated.
4. Scoop 1 tbsp of the mixture and compress until the ingredients form a nugget.
Form it into a rectangle with ½ inch height for easy cooking.
5. Repeat the previous step for the rest of the ingredients.
6. Prepare a frying pan and pour-in some cooking oil. Apply low to medium heat and wait until it is hot.
7. Add the nuggets and fry them until it is golden brown.
8. Flip the nuggets and let it fry once more. Do this until all the sides are golden brown.
9. Prepare a serving tray or serving plate and place a paper towel on it to absorb the excess oil.
