Buko Salad

Ingredients:

• 2 cups young coconut meat • 1 can evaporated milk (370ml)
• 1 cup uncooked tapioca pearls (sago) • 1 can condensed milk (390g)
• 1 packet gelatine (25 grams) • 1 canned of mixed fruit
• 2 cups coconut water • 1/4 cup sugar
• food coloring (red, green, yellow)
Procedure:
1. Separate the mixed fruit from its juice. Set aside.
2. Boil six cups of water in a pot. Put into it a cup of tapioca pearl and cook for 15 minutes.
Put off the heat and cover with a fitting pan lid to cook it further. Set it aside.
3. Pour another six cups of water into the pot. Dissolve a pack of gelatine powder
completely and a quarter cup sugar. Cook in a medium-low heat and bring to boil.
4. Once boiling, slowly transfer into three separate containers. For each container,
add a pinch of food coloring and stir. Let it cool and set. Then, cut into small cubes. Set aside.
5. In a large mixing bowl, combine the two cups of young coconut meat with the colorful
gelatin cubes, cooked tapioca pearls and mixed fruit. Stir evenly.
6. Next, add the two cups of coconut water, a can of evaporated milk, and a can of
condensed milk. Give it a good mix. Refrigerate or freeze. Enjoy your cup of fresh Buko Salad!
