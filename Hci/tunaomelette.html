Tuna Melt Omelette

Ingredients:
1 can tuna
3 pcs eggs
1 pc Onion
4 cloves garlic
Cooking oil
Salt and Ground Black Pepper to taste
Procedure:
1. Prepare the tuna by sautéing. Heat oil in a pan and then sauté garlic and onion.
Add corned beef. Continue to sauté for 3 to 5 minutes. Remove from the pan and let it cool down.
2. Crack the eggs and place in a large mixing bowl. Add the salt and pepper. Beat until all the ingredients are well incorporated.
3. Add sautéed tuna. Stir to mix the ingredients. Note: Make sure that the tuna has cooled down before you execute this step.
4. Heat a frying pan. Add 1 tablespoon cooking oil or use a cooking oil spray and spray oil on the pan.
5. Scoop around ½ to ¾ cups of the tuna and egg mixture and pour in the pan. Cook for 3 minutes on medium heat.
6. Use a spatula to flip the omelette and cook the other side for 2 to 3 minutes.
7. Transfer to a serving plate. Serve.
