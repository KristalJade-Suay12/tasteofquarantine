Lumpiang Corned Beef

Ingredients:
2 tbsp Cooking Oil Salt and Pepper
4 cloves Garlic 12 pcs Lumpia Wrapper
1 pc Onion 1 cup Cooking Oil
1 pc Potato Spiced Vinegar/Ketchup
1 pc Carrot
2 cans Corned Beef
Procedure:
1. Saut√© garlic, onion and potato in oil for 5 minutes.
Add carrot and corned beef. Cook for another 2 minutes.
2. Stir it and season with salt and pepper.
Transfer on a wide plate to cool down completely.
3. Assemble 2 tbsp of the corned beef mixture with the lumpia wrapper.
4. Fry in preheated oil until golden brown.
Transfer on a cooling rack to drain excess oil.
5. Cut in half and serve with spiced vinegar or ketchup for sauce.
