Meaty (Pork)Fried Rice

Ingredients:

4 cups Cooked but Cold Rice
2 Garlic cloves
1 onion
8 ounces Ground Pork
3 tbsp oil
1 tsp Salt
1/2 cup Frozen Peas and Carrots
2 tbsp Sor Sauce
Procedure:
1. Dice 1 small yellow onion.
2. Heat 1 tablespoon of the oil in a wok or large non-stick frying pan over medium-high heat until shimmering.
Add 2 large eggs and gently scramble with a rubber spatula until just softly set, 1 to 2 minutes.
Transfer to a bowl or plate and set aside.
3. Add the onion, 8 ounces ground pork, 1 teaspoon salt, and 1/4 teaspoon black pepper to the pan.
Cook, breaking up the meat with a wooden spoon into small pieces, until the pork is
cooked through and the onion is tender, about 5 minutes.
4. Add the remaining 2 tablespoons oil and let heat for a few seconds.
Add 4 cups cold cooked rice, 1/2 cup frozen peas and carrots, and 2 tablespoons soy sauce,
, and stir-fry until the rice is heated through, 2 to 3 minutes. Return the eggs to the pan.
