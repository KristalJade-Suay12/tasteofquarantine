Tuna Sisig

Ingredients:
2 cans tuna
3 pcs Red and Green Chili
2 pc Onion
2 cloves garlic
3 tablespoon Cooking oil
Salt and Ground Black Pepper to taste
1/4 cup Mayonaise 1 tsp Butter
Procedure:
1. Prepare the tuna by draining the liquid. You can place it in a cheese cloth and squeeze until the liquid comes-out.
2. Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a pan. Pan fry the tuna until it turns light to medium brown.
3. Add garlic and onion. Continue to saute until the garlic and onion becomes soft.
4. Add chili pepper. Saute for 2 more minutes. Season with ground black pepper, and soy sauce.
5. Stir and cook for 1 minute. Add half of the chicharon and put the mayonnaise into the pot. Mix well. Set aside.
6. Melt butter in sizzling plate (metal plate), put the cooked tuna sisig on the plate.
6. Use a spatula to flip the omelette and cook the other side for 2 to 3 minutes.
7. Top with remaining chicharon.
