Mango Jelly Salad

Ingredients:

• 10 cups water • ½ cup tapioca pearls
• 1 sachet mango-flavored gelatin powder • 1 kilo ripe mangoes
• nata de coco, drained • 2 packs all-purpose cream
• 1 can condensed milk (390g) or 500ml heavy cream
Procedure:
1. Boil 5 cups of water in a pan. Add the tapioca pearls and cook for 15 minutes.
When cook, transfer in a bowl and soak in cold water to prevent sticking until ready to use.
2. Add 5 cups of water in a pot. Add the mango-flavored gelatin powder and dissolve well.
Let it boil. When boiling, remove from heat and transfer to a tray or molder.
3. Slice the ripe mangoes and cut into cubes. Set aside.
4. When the mango gelatin is firm, cut into cubes. Set aside.
5. In the large mixing bowl, add the chopped mangoes, gelatin cubes,
5. In a large mixing bowl, combine the two cups of young coconut meat with the colorful
tapioca pearls, nata de coco and mix well.
6. Add the all-purpose cream or heavy cream. Add the condensed milk.
Mix well and chill the dessert for at least 5 hours.
