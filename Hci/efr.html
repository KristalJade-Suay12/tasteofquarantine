Garlic Fried Rice

Ingredients:

4 cups Cooked but Cold Rice
2 Garlic cloves
2 Eggs
2 tbsp Dark Soy Sauce
1 tbsp Scallions (optional)
1 tbsp oil
1/4 tsp Salt
Procedure:
1.---Beat the eggs and minced the garlic.
2.---Add 1 tbsp of the oil in a large wok.
Add the garlic and cook, stirring regularly on a medium
heat for 30 seconds until the garlic starts to turn brown and translucent.
3.---Add the beaten eggs and keep mixing it so that it doesn't cooked completely scrambled egg.
4.---Add the rice and turn up the heat to high.
5.---Use a spatula to toss everything together and to ensure the rice doesn't stick to the bottom
of the pan. Keep moving the rice around so that it's all getting reheated.
6.---Pour in the soy sauce, sprinkle the Â¼ tsp of salt and mix it all together.
7.---Top with spring onions (scallions).
