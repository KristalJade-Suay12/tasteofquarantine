Sardine Patty

Ingredients:
2 cans Sardines
2 pcs eggs
1 pc Onion
2-3 Cloves of Garlic
1 pc Carrots
Cooking oil
Salt and GPepper
Procedure:
1. Grate the carrots. And chop the onion and garlic.
2. Combine all the ingredients in a large bowl. Mix it all together.
3. Use two tablespoon of sardines mixture to make a patty.
4. Turn on the gas stove on low medium heat. Add cooking oil in a deep pan.
Once it is hot, add the patties. Let it cook for 3 to 5 minutes or until it turn dark brown.
Flip it and repeat the process.
5. Prepare a paper towel in the plate.
Once it is cook, transfer the patties into the plate to help drain the excess oil.
