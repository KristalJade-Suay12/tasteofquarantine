Sardine Omelette

Ingredients:
1 can Sardines
2 pcs eggs
1 pc Onion
Cooking oil
Salt and Ground Black Pepper
Procedure:
1. Open a can of sardines and separate sardines from the tomato sauce.
Remove the fishbone of the sardines. Set aside the tomato sauce.
2. In a bowl put in eggs, a pinch of salt, a dash of ground pepper, onion and sardines.
3. Mash the sardines and mix all the prepared ingredients until texture softens.
4. Heat pan and drizzle with cooking oil.
5. Scoop a portion of the mixture then fry in medium heat until the bottom becomes firm.
Flip the omelette to cook the other side. Side note, you can pour all the mixture once and
spread it over the pan to minimize the time for cooking or follow our given procedure.
6. Repeat step 5 for the rest of the mixture, in case you're cooking the mixture in portions.
7. Once done frying all of the mixture, transfer on a plate
Another side note, garnish the tomato sauce over the sardines omelette or serve it separately.
