Sardine Nuggets

Ingredients:
2 can Sardines
2-3 Cloves of Garlic
1 pc Onion
1 Tomato
2 tsp Calamansi
1/2 tsp Salt and Pepper
Cooking oil
Procedure:
1. Heat the oil in a pan.
2. Saut√© the garlic and onion
3. When the onion becomes soft, add the tomato and cook for 3 minutes.
4. Pour-in the sardines in tomato sauce. Stir and let boil.
5. Cook for 5 minutes in low to medium heat. You may add a little water if needed.
6. Add-in the lemon juice, ground black pepper, and salt or fish sauce. Stir and cook for a minute more.
7. Turn off heat and then transfer to a serving bowl.
