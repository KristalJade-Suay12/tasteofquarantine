Sardine FishBall

Ingredients:
1 can Sardines 1/2 cup water
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour 2 tbsp spring Onion
2-3 Cloves of Garlic
1 pc Onion
1/2 tsp Salt and Pepper
Cooking oil
Procedure:
1. Remove the fishbone of sardines.
2. Mash the sardines (sauce included). Then add onion, garlic, spring onions. Mix well.
3. Add all-purpose flour and baking powder. Mix well. Then add water small amount at a time
until you achieve the right consistency (thick and not too runny). Season with salt and pepper to taste.
4. Heat some oil in a deep pan for deep-frying. Using two spoons, shape the mixture into balls.
Then drop to a hot oil. Fry until golden brown.
5. Enjoy with homemade fishball sauce.
