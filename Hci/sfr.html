Seafood (Shrimp) Fried Rice

Ingredients:

4 cups Cooked but Cold Rice
2 Garlic cloves
1 onion
1 lb Shrimp
4 tbsp butter
1 tbsp oil
1/4 tsp Salt
Chopped Green Onion
Procedure:
1. In a pan heat the oil.
2. Add in the shrimp into the pan.
3. Sprinkle the seafood with 1 tbsp of soy sauce
4. Sauté until shrimp are completely cooked. Do not overcook.
5. Remove shrimp. Set aside.
6. Add 2 tbsp of the butter into the wok.
7. Once the butter melts, add in the onion sauté until slightly caramelized; about 2-3 mins
8. Add in the garlic and sauté for 30 secs. Then add the rice
9. Taste and add salt and soy sauce as needed. Stir in the rice
10. Lower the heat and return the shrimp to the wok
11. Stir and fluff until seafood is reheated.
12. Garnish with chopped green onions.
