Vegetable Fried Rice

Ingredients:

4 cups Cooked but Cold Rice
2 Garlic cloves
3 Eggs
2 tbsp Dark Soy Sauce
1 pack Frozen Mixed Vegetables
2 tbsp oil
1/4 tsp Salt
Procedure:
1. Prepare frozen vegetables.
2. Scramble the eggs in a frying pan, making sure to break the egg up into small pieces.
Add the mixed vegetables.
3. Heat oil in large skillet over medium-high heat.
Add garlic and cook for 5 minutes or until onion is cooked and translucent.
4. Add the rice, scrambled eggs and mixed vegetables.
5. Add soy sauce and salt to taste.
of the pan. Keep moving the rice around so that it's all getting reheated.
Continue to saute, stirring occasionally, for an additional 3 minutes.
6. Then remove from heat. Serve immediately.
