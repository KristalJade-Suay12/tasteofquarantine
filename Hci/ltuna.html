Lumpiang Tuna

Ingredients:
2 cans Tuna
1/4 tsp Black Pepper
1 pc Onion
4 cloves garlic
1/4 cup Carrots
Beaten egg or water for sealing edges
Sweet Chili Sauce for dipping
Procedure:
1. Drain water or oil from canned tuna.
2. In a large bowl, combine all ingredients and stir until well combined.
3. Prepare the lumpia or spring roll wrapper by carefully peeling individual wrappers apart.
4. Lay the wrapper flat on a clean surface. Place about 2-3 tbsp of filling on the edge of the wrapper closest to
you, leaving about 1 1/2 space at both ends. Continue rolling the rest of the filling and wrappers.
5. Heat cooking oil in a deep pan and drop the lumpia in hot oil. Do not overcrowd the pan.
6. Fry until crisp and golden. Remove from oil and drain. Lumpia can be cut into two (diagonally) to make smaller rolls.
7. Serve with chili sauce.
