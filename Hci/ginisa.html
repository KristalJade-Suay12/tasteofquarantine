Ginisang Corned Beef

Ingredients:
2 tbsp cooking oil 1 pc egg(optional)
4 cloves Garlic
1 pc Onion
1 pc Potato
1 can Corned Beef
Salt and Pepper
Procedure:
1. Saut√© garlic and onion in oil until golden brown.
2. Add potato and cook until light golden brown. Add corned beef. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
3. Add the egg and continue to stir for at least 1min.
4. Transfer on a serving plate and serve.
