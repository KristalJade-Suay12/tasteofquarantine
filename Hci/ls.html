Lumpiang Sardinas

Ingredients:
2 can Sardines 1/2 cup water
1 pcs Carrot 2 tbsp spring Onion
2-3 Cloves of Garlic Malungay Leaves Optional
1 pc Onion 1 egg
1/2 tsp Salt and Pepper Small Lumpia Wrappers
Cooking oil Water for lumpia seal
Procedure:
1. Open the cans of sardines and separate the sardines from the tomato sauce. And remove the fishbone of sardines.
2. In a large mixing bowl, transfer the sardines and cut into small pieces. Add the carrots, onions,
garlic, onion leeks, all-purpose flour and egg. Mix the ingredients well. Season with salt and pepper. Mix well.
3. Lay a piece of lumpia wrapper in a clean flat space. Put a tablespoon of the sardine filling.
4. Roll the lumpia. Make sure that the sides are closed. Seal the ends of the lumpia wrapper with water or egg white.
Repeat this process until the sardine filling is fully consumed.
5. Heat some oil in a pan under medium heat for deep-frying.
Fry the lumpiang sardinas in batches until the wrapper is golden brown.
Roll the lumpia pieces into the pan to ensure that all sides are cooked.
Transfer the cooked lumpiang sardinas in a strainer or on a tissue paper to drain the excess oil.
